Lasagna Recipe 3

bla bla recipe of a lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Put the tomatoes in the blender
  3. Put the tomato sauce on the pasta
  4. Put grated cheese on the sauce
  5. Add more pasta layers to taste
  6. Add MORE CHEESE on top
  7. Cook at 350F for 45m
